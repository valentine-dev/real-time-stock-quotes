Monthly DRIP with Discount in Canada

Is there a public company in Canada that has all of the following?

  • cash dividend EACH month
  • Dividend Re-Investment Plan (DRIP)
  • a discount rate applicable to its DRIP
Luckily, I find such a company: Northland Power (NPI) at https://www.northlandpower.com/.

Northland Power common share dividend is currently paid $0.10 monthly with discount rate applicable to its DRIP from 0% to 3%.

Enjoy it!
