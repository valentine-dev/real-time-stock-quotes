Monthly DRIP with Discount in Canada 💗 Donations

Is there a public company in Canada that has all of the following?

  • 🸏 cash dividend EACH month
  • 🸏 Dividend Re-Investment Plan (“DRIP”)
  • 🸏 a discount rate applicable to its DRIP
Luckily, I find such a company: Northland Power at https://www.northlandpower.com/!

Northland Power common share dividend is currently paid $0.10 monthly with discount rate applicable to its Dividend Re-Investment Plan (“DRIP”) from 0% to 3%.
