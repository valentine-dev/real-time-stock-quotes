Monthly DRIP with Discount in Canada 💗 Donations

Is there a public company that:

  • delivers cash dividend EACH month AND
  • has Dividend Re-Investment Plan (“DRIP”) AND
  • has a discount rate applicable to its DRIP?
Luckily, I find such a company: Northland Power at https://www.northlandpower.com/!

Northland Power annual common share dividend is currently $1.20, paid at the rate of $0.10 per month with discount rate applicable to its Dividend Re-Investment Plan (“DRIP”) from 0% to 3%.
